Privacy Policy – SafeScroll

Effective Date: 22 February 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Year of birth

This information is securely stored in Firebase and is used only for user identification, account management, subscription management, customer support, and basic personalization of the app experience. We do not sell or share your personal information for advertising purposes.

2. Accessibility Service Permission

SafeScroll uses Accessibility Service strictly to provide core safety features. It is used to:

  • Detect and block adult or explicit content in real time
  • Detect when restricted apps are opened to apply focus limits
  • Automatically exit restricted apps using system navigation actions
  • Monitor app protection settings to prevent unauthorized removal

SafeScroll may take temporary screenshots to analyze content using on-device AI.

Important:

  • Images are processed locally on your device
  • Screenshots are never stored
  • Screen data is never uploaded
  • Screen content is never shared

All content detection runs entirely offline on your device.

3. Data Storage & Security

Personal account data (email, phone number, year of birth) is securely stored in Firebase. Screen data and AI detection results are processed locally and are not stored on external servers. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information.

4. Children’s Privacy

SafeScroll may be installed by parents on devices used by children. We do not knowingly collect additional personal data from children beyond account information provided by the parent or guardian. Parents are responsible for supervising their child’s device usage.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information only with secure service providers (such as Firebase) or if required by law.

6. Your Control

You can revoke permissions at any time in device settings, request deletion of your account data, or uninstall the app at any time.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: contact@safescroll.app

This Privacy Policy applies specifically to the SafeScroll mobile application.
